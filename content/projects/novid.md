# NOVID

During the early months of the COVID-19 pandemic, I joined NOVID as an iOS Engineer to help build the world's first pre-exposure contact tracing solution. Unlike conventional Bluetooth-only approaches, NOVID used network theory to provide earlier warnings of potential exposure.
